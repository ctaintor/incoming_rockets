Incoming Rocket Alerts

How do I use this?

Where does this data come from?

The data from this is pulled from Home Front Command. There is no guarantee that this will definitively work, so use this in addition to listening for sirens.

How do I find my warning area?

You can find your warning area by searching for your city here. It will say something like "You belongs to the warning area: Dan 157." So, your warning area is '157' in this case.

Can you give me some example warning areas?

  • Beer Sheva - 292
  • Haifa - 75
  • Modiin - 181
  • Jerusalem - 194
  • Tel Aviv-Yafo - 157
You can also test what happens when there is an alert by setting the warning area to 613.

Does this work in my browser?

I have tested this in the latest versions of desktop Chrome and Firefox. It should also work in versions of Internet Explorer newer than IE8. Finally, the sound will not play on many Android devices. You should not use this as an alert system on a mobile phone!
